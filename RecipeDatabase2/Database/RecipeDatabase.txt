NAMEBIBIMBOP
INGR8 oz extra firm tofu, drained
INGR1/3 cup water
INGR1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
INGR2 tsp sugar
INGR2 tsp minced garlic
INGR1 tsp ginger
INGR1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
INGR1 cup julienne-cut carrot
INGR2 Tbsp soy sauce
INGR4 Tbsp sesame oil
INGR3 cups hot cooked rice
INGR1 cup bean sprouts
INGR5 oz sliced shitake mushroom caps
INGR9 oz fresh baby spinach
INGR1 tsp butter
INGR4 large eggs
INGRKorean Chili paste to taste
INGRsalt to taste
STEPCut tofu into 3/4 inch thick slices.  Place in a single layer on several layers of paper towels.  Let stand 30 minutes.
STEPCombine 1/3 cup water, vinegar, 1 tsp sugar, 1/2 tsp garlic, 1/2 tsp ginger, and crushed red pepper in a small sauce pan.  Bring to a boil.  Add carrot and remove from heat.  Let stand 30 minutes.
STEPCut tofu into 3/4 inch cubes.  Place tofu in a medium bowl.  Combine 1 tsp sugar, 1/2 tsp garlic, 1/2 tsp ginger, soy sauce, and 1 Tbsp oil.  Whisk.
STEPAdd 1 Tbsp soy sauce mixture to tofu and toss gently.  Let stand 15 minues.
STEPHeat a large nonstick skillet over medium high heat.
STEPAdd 1 tsp oil and 1 1/2 tsp soy sauce mixture, and bean sprouts to pan.  Saute for 1 minute.
STEPRemove sprouts from pan and keep warm.
STEPAdd 1 tsp oil, mushrooms, saute 2 minutes.  Add 1 1/2 tsp soy sauce mixture and saute 1 minute.
STEPRemove mushrooms from pan and keep warm.
STEPAdd tsp oil to pan.  Saute tofu for 7 minutes until golden brown.
STEPRemoe tofu and keep warm.
STEPAdd 1 tsp oil.  Add 1 tsp garlic, and remaining soy sauce mixture.  Saute 30 seconds.  Add spinach.  Saute for 1 minute until spinach wilts.
STEPRemove spinach and keep warm.
STEPMelt butter in the pan.  Cook eggs sunny side up until whites are set ~4 min.
STEPPlace 3/4 cup rice in a bowl.  Top evenly with carrots, spinach, sprouts, mushrooms and tofu.  Top each with 1 egg.
STEPAdd chili paste and salt to taste.
ENDRECIPE

STEPStir dry ingredients into wet. Dough will be very sticky
STEPSprinkle 1/2 cup cake flour on a work surface.  Kneed the dough on it until all the flour is incorporated.
STEPCoat a large bowl with cooking spray.  Add the dough to the bowl.  
STEPCoat with cooking spray and cover with plastic wrap.
STEPLet rise at room temp until it doubles.  1 1/4 to 2 hours.
STEPCoat a 9x13 metal baking pan with cooking spray.
STEPTurn the dough out onto a lightly floured  surface.  Pat it to roughly a 7x10 inch rectangle.
STEPUsing a sharp knife cut into 24 pieces.
STEPFor each piece, gather and pinch the bottom corners together. 
STEPPlace the ball bottom down roll lightly into a ball until the edges of the ball tight.
